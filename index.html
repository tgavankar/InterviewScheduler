Frequently Asked Questions


  1. What is ScheduleMatch?
    1. A quick and easy way to schedule interview times for all your interview candidates.
  2. Benefits of using ScheduleMatch?
    1. Eliminate back and forth emails between you and your candidate.
    2. Find matching times with just a click of a button.
    3. Fire your HR and cut costs.
  3. How to use ScheduleMatch?
    1. Fill in the first and last name of your interviewer.
    2. Enter in your interviewer's Calendar ID. The calendar must be set to PUBLIC.
    3. To get your Calendar Id, go to the "Calendar Settings" of your public calendar. Your id is next to "Calendar Address"
    4. Your Calendar ID should follow this format: r5p25tev3v42r11u242u959ev8@group.calendar.google.com
    5. Enter in a location, start, and end time for your interviews.
    6. Enter in your candidate names and their Calendar IDs. Their Calendar IDs must also be public.
    7. Hit submit and watch the magic happen.
